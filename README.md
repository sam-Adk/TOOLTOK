## 1. Business Overview
**Name:** ToolTok  
**Type:** Mobile App / Social Media Platform for Construction & DIY  
**Founder / CEO:** Samuel Adikah  

**Mission:**  
To create a platform where DIY enthusiasts, builders, and homeowners can discover, share, and learn construction and hardware skills daily.

**Vision:**  
To become the leading short-form video platform for DIY, construction, and hardware content in Kenya and Africa.

**Services Offered:**  
- Short-form construction & DIY videos  
- Tool and material reviews  
- Mini-project tutorials  
- E-commerce for tools & materials *(optional)*  

---

## 2. Target Audience
- DIY enthusiasts and hobbyists  
- Builders, carpenters, electricians, and construction workers  
- Homeowners and renovators  
- Hardware/tool brands looking for promotion  
- Students studying construction, architecture, or design  

---

## 3. Revenue Model
1. **Advertising / Sponsored Content:**  
   - Tool brands, hardware shops, paint companies, construction services  
2. **Affiliate Marketing / E-commerce Integration:**  
   - Sell tools, nails, hammers, drills, or materials featured in videos  
3. **Premium Content / Courses:**  
   - Paid tutorials for advanced carpentry, electrical work, or specialized construction skills  
4. **Brand Partnerships:**  
   - Collaborate with vendors for featured product placements or promotions  
5. **In-App Purchases / Subscriptions:**  
   - Unlock advanced features, remove ads, or access premium projects
